Move Beyond Demos. Ship Production Systems.

Minor / no change
US Brochure · Original

Agentic AI has moved past the prototype. Companies don't need another chatbot demo. They need engineers who can scope a problem with a customer, build a production-grade agentic system, deploy it inside the customer's environment, and own it end to end.

The Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) sits at the intersection of software engineering, customer-facing delivery, and AI deployment. The FDE owns production code that runs inside the customer's environment, covering orchestration, safety, evaluation, cost control, and operations.

This program is not about “using AI.” It trains the engineering judgment senior AI roles are hired for: deciding when to use agents versus deterministic systems, selecting the right design patterns, and justifying architectural trade-offs.

It then extends that depth with the distinctly FDE craft: customer discovery, problem framing, scoping & commercials, enterprise wiring & governance, operations & customer handover, and closes with FDE-specific interview preparation.
